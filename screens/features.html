Manage your schedule

At Workjeje, we believe everyone should work in their own time. That is why we have a schedule manager in place for each professional. Your schedule is public. This way we know when you are busy or unavailable to work at the moment. The schedule manager also helps you organize your work.

Show off your work

At Workjeje, we believe every professional should have a solid proof of quality which is why we believe you should leverage the power of a great profile. Use your gallery to show off what you can do and why you are the best. This helps to increase your chances of getting hired.

Manage contracts

Our contract manager helps organize your contracts. You can either accept, reject or renegotiate contract terms using the contract manager. We do not charge you till a contract is accepted. You can only accept a contract once, so make sure you have read through the terms properly before accepting.

Manage and accept bids

Workjeje gives professionals the opportunity to pitch themselves to potential clients. You can manage applications for jobs posted and either accept or ignore bids. Accepting a bid sends a contract to a professional of your choosing which they can either accept, reject or renegotiate.

At Workjeje we believe improvement is not possible without feedback from clients. That is why we give clients the opportunity to tell us what they think about each professional they work with. This aids improvement and growth on the part of the professionals and helps with choice making on the part of the clients
