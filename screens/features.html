Features
Get more done from the comfort of your couch.
Show off your work
At Work jeje, we believe every professional should have proof of quality which is why you should leverage the power of a great profile to attract your clients. Use the gallery to show off what you can do and why you are the best. A good profile increases your chances of getting hired
Manage your schedule
At Work jeje, we believe everyone should work in their own time. That is why we have a schedule manager in place for each professional. Your schedule is public. This way we know when you are busy or unavailable to work. The schedule manager also helps you organize your work activities.
Find Jobs Nearby
Work jeje collects your location in real-time to personalize your gig searching experience. Work jeje opens doors to lots of job opportunities for you based on your capabilities and reputation. This means that the more skilled you are (which is evident in your rating/reviews), the easier it is for you to be contracted because we prioritize the best
Manage contracts
Our contract manager helps organize your contracts. You can either accept, reject or renegotiate contract terms using the contract manager. We do not charge you till a contract is accepted. You can only accept a contract once, so make sure you have read through the terms properly before accepting.
Rate each provider
At Work jeje we believe improvement is not possible without feedback from clients. That is why we allow clients to tell us what they think about each service provider they work with. This aids improvement and growth on the part of the professionals and helps with choice-making on the part of the clients
Manage and accept bids
Work jeje allows professionals to pitch themselves to potential clients. You can manage requests for jobs posted and either accept or ignore bids. Accepting a bid sends a contract to a professional of your choosing which they can either accept, reject or renegotiate.
